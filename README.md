## üéÆ Usage in Nostalgia

These music files are automatically downloaded when you first launch the Nostalgia game. The download happens in the background while you play, and tracks become available as they finish downloading.

You can access the in-game radio through the music menu to switch between stations:
- **FM Lo-Fi** - Relaxing lo-fi beats
- **Outrun FM** - Synthwave/retrowave vibes  
- **Vaporwave Frequency** - Aesthetic vaporwave tracks
